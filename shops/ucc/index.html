UCC is a Japanese based coffee shop that has branches in different parts of the Philippines, including Cebu. You can find UCC in Ayala Terraces. Not only do they have a wide variety of of coffee and teas to choose from. They also have a menu for breakfast, lunch and dinner. If you're looking for good coffee shops in Ayala, you should definitely go there.
